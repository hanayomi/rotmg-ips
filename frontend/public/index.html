Welcome to the Server IP Tool

This tool allows you to easily copy the IP addresses of different Realm of the Mad God servers. Simply click on any server's button, and its IP address will be copied to your clipboard.

How to Use

To use the tool, simply navigate through the list of servers above, click on the server you want, and the IP address will be automatically copied to your clipboard. You can then paste it wherever you need, whether it's for server configuration or checking connectivity.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I copy a server IP? Click on any of the server buttons, and the IP will be copied automatically.
  • Why are some servers labeled as [BETA]? These servers are in a testing phase and may not be as stable as regular servers.
  • Can I suggest new servers? Yes, feel free to reach out to us to suggest new servers or regions.
